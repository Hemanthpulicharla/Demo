The UPSC Civil Services Exam is a highly competitive and coveted exam that attracts thousands of aspiring candidates every year. It's a challenging exam that requires a great deal of hard work, discipline, and dedication to succeed.

One of the key decisions that candidates have to make as they begin their preparation is whether or not to take coaching. Coaching institutes can be expensive and time-consuming, but they can also provide expert guidance and structured study plans that can be helpful for those preparing for the exam.
